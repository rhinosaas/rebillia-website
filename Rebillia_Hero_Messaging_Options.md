# Rebillia Homepage Hero — Messaging Options

*Created: 2026-03-24*

---

## Background & Thought Process

The current Rebillia homepage hero positions the product as **"Agent-Native Monetization Infrastructure"** with the headline **"The monetization layer for AI-operated businesses."** While forward-looking, this positioning may alienate our core audience — ecommerce merchants who need subscription billing — and doesn't clearly communicate what Rebillia actually does.

### Direction
We explored a **hybrid approach**: lead with practical subscription billing value that anyone can understand, then weave in AI/MCP capabilities as a differentiator rather than the headline. The goal is to make Rebillia feel accessible to non-technical store owners while signaling to developers and AI builders that this is infrastructure they can build on.

### Constraints
- No mention of specific ecommerce platforms (BigCommerce, WooCommerce) in the hero — keep it broad (except where noted in Option 3)
- No listing of specific features (multi-gateway, dunning, etc.) — keep it high-level
- MCP server is the one technical feature worth calling out as a differentiator
- Use first-person voice ("we") to feel human and approachable
- CTAs stay as: **"Start Free Trial"** and **"Schedule a Walkthrough"**

>
> **Headline:** Subscription billing that grows with you

> **Subtitle:** Recurring revenue for ecommerce, SaaS, and services — with multi-gateway billing, smart dunning, and an AI-ready API that lets agents manage subscriptions alongside your team.

*Positions Rebillia as an engine/platform rather than just a tool.*

>

## Option 3: Bridge Positioning (with specific platforms)

*Leads with subscriptions, hooks with AI-ready. Names specific platforms for SEO and credibility.*

> **Eyebrow:** SUBSCRIPTION BILLING INFRASTRUCTURE
>
> **Headline:** Subscription infrastructure. Now AI-ready.
>
> **Subtitle:** The billing engine powering recurring revenue across BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and custom stacks — with an MCP server that makes every subscription operation available to AI agents.

## Option 8: Outcome-First

*Leads with what the customer gets. AI is the enabler, not the headline.*

> **Eyebrow:** RECURRING REVENUE INFRASTRUCTURE
>
> **Headline:** Grow subscriptions. Let AI handle the rest.
>

## Option 9: Dual-Audience (Ecommerce Owners + Developers/AI Builders)

*Explicitly speaks to two audiences: non-technical store owners AND developers building with AI. Focused on speed to launch and speed to monetize.*

> **Eyebrow:** LAUNCH SUBSCRIPTIONS IN MINUTES
>
> **Headline:** The fastest way to launch subscription billing.
>
> **Subtitle:** Online store? Install our app and start recurring revenue today — no code required. Building with AI? Connect our API and MCP server and ship billing before lunch.
>
> Stop configuring. Start monetizing.

**Best for:** When you need to clearly serve two audiences in one hero. The "online store / building with AI" split makes it immediately clear who this is for. Speed-to-value is the core message for both groups.
