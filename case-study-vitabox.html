CASE STUDY

VitaBox Supplements

How a wellness subscription brand cut churn by 40% and slashed support tickets by 60% with Rebillia.

Health & Supplements BigCommerce Subscription Boxes
ABOUT THE COMPANY

Company Overview

VitaBox is a health and supplements brand offering curated wellness boxes with vitamins, supplements, and wellness products. Their subscription model delivers personalized wellness packages to customers monthly, helping people stay consistent with their health routines.

THE CHALLENGE

High Churn and Overwhelming Support Requests

VitaBox was experiencing significant growing pains as their subscriber base expanded. Two critical issues threatened their business:

  • High involuntary churn from failed payments with no automated recovery process
  • Overwhelming customer support requests for basic subscription modifications like skipping a month, changing products, or updating payment methods
  • No self-service options meant every subscription change required a support ticket
  • Revenue was being lost silently as failed payments went unrecovered
THE SOLUTION

Smart Dunning, Self-Service Portal & Flexible Management

VitaBox deployed Rebillia with a focus on automated payment recovery and customer empowerment. The comprehensive implementation addressed both churn and support challenges simultaneously:

Smart Dunning

Intelligent payment retry sequences with customized email notifications to recover failed payments automatically

Self-Service Portal

Customer portal for managing subscriptions, skipping deliveries, swapping products, and updating payment details

Flexible Management

Flexible subscription options including pause, skip, swap, and frequency changes without contacting support
