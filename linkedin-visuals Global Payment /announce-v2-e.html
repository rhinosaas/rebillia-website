Rebillia partners with Global Payments through PayFabric to deliver enterprise payment processing inside an adaptive subscription control plane.
Enterprise payments without adaptive billing create rigidity. Adaptive billing without enterprise payments creates risk.

This partnership solves both — connecting Rebillia's component-based subscription engine with one of the world's largest payment infrastructures.
"Subscriptions were built for predictability. The AI era requires adaptability."
Subscriptions evolve across Address, Money, Product, Customer, Channel, and Time — without cancellation or reset. Payments execute securely through tokenized, multi-currency, enterprise infrastructure.
What This Enables
Tokenized Vaulting — secure credentials
Multi-Currency — global processing
Real-Time Fraud — active detection
AI Governance — shared control
Why It Matters
AI agents are starting to manage subscriptions. Billing is no longer a back-office function — it's the control plane of the customer relationship. This integration makes that control plane programmable, secure, and lifecycle-aware.
