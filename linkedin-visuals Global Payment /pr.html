Rebillia Partners with Global Payments to Deliver Enterprise-Grade Payment Processing for Subscription Businesses

Partnership brings Global Payments' PayFabric platform to Rebillia customers — ecommerce merchants, SaaS companies, and AI-powered businesses — enabling multi-currency recurring billing, tokenized card vaulting, and preferred processing rates across 170+ countries

March 30, 2026

Rebillia, the adaptive subscription billing infrastructure platform for ecommerce, SaaS, and AI-native businesses, today announced a strategic partnership with Global Payments (NYSE: GPN), one of the world's leading payment technology companies, naming Global Payments' PayFabric as its preferred payment gateway. The partnership delivers enterprise-grade payment processing purpose-built for recurring revenue businesses of every kind.

Through Global Payments' PayFabric platform, Rebillia customers now gain access to payment infrastructure that processes over 50 billion transactions per year, bringing the scale, security, and global reach that modern subscription businesses demand, whether they're shipping physical products, delivering software, or powering the next wave of AI-built applications.

The partnership addresses one of the most persistent challenges in subscription businesses: payment reliability. For ecommerce merchants running subscribe-and-save programs, SaaS founders scaling their user base, or AI vibe coders shipping their first paid product, every failed payment represents revenue already earned that walks out the door. Industry data shows that 10-20% of annual subscriber churn stems from involuntary payment failures alone.

"Whether you're an ecommerce merchant, a SaaS founder, or an AI developer shipping your first subscription product, you need payment infrastructure that's as reliable as the recurring revenue you're building. Partnering with Global Payments gives our customers access to world-class payment processing, preferred rates, and global reach, all natively integrated with the billing engine they already rely on."

Snir Avidan, CEO of Rebillia

What the Partnership Delivers

With Global Payments' PayFabric platform integrated into Rebillia's subscription billing infrastructure, businesses across ecommerce, SaaS, and AI can now:

  • Process payments globally: Accept recurring payments in 100+ currencies across 170+ countries, unlocking international subscription revenue without additional payment integrations.
  • Reduce failed payments: Leverage tokenized card vaulting for seamless recurring charges, combined with Rebillia's smart dunning engine, to recover revenue that would otherwise be lost to payment declines.
  • Strengthen security: Operate with PCI DSS Level 1 compliance, the highest level of payment security certification, along with built-in fraud prevention tools.
  • Access preferred rates: Rebillia merchants receive preferred processing rates through the partnership, reducing the cost of accepting recurring payments at scale.
  • Simplify the payment stack: Consolidate subscription billing and enterprise payment processing into a single, integrated solution, eliminating middleware and reducing technical complexity.

"Businesses of all sizes deserve enterprise-grade payment infrastructure to power their subscription revenue. This partnership with Rebillia brings PayFabric's capabilities directly to the fast-growing ecosystem of ecommerce merchants, SaaS companies, and AI-native builders who need reliable, scalable recurring payment processing."

Global Payments Spokesperson

Built for Every Subscription Business

As Rebillia's preferred payment gateway, PayFabric will be the recommended processing option for new customers across all segments. Rebillia continues to support 7+ payment gateways including Stripe, PayPal, Authorize.net, Square, Braintree, and CyberSource, giving businesses the flexibility to optimize payment routing and avoid single-gateway lock-in.

Rebillia serves three distinct customer segments, each now benefiting from Global Payments' infrastructure:

  • Ecommerce merchants running subscribe-and-save, subscription boxes, and recurring fulfillment on BigCommerce (where Rebillia is the top-rated subscription app), with WooCommerce and Shopify support expanding.
  • SaaS and digital services companies needing flexible recurring billing with usage-based pricing, multi-gateway support, and smart dunning out of the box.
  • AI agents and vibe coders who need the fastest path from idea to live subscription payments. Rebillia's API-first architecture and MCP server integration let AI-powered tools and developers launch paid products in minutes, not months.

About Rebillia

Rebillia is adaptive subscription billing infrastructure for ecommerce, SaaS, and AI-native businesses. The platform enables businesses to launch and scale recurring revenue with subscribe-and-save, subscription boxes, usage-based billing, multi-gateway recurring payments, smart dunning, and a customer self-service portal. Rebillia integrates deeply with leading ecommerce platforms including BigCommerce (where it is the top-rated subscription app), with WooCommerce and Shopify expanding, while its REST API and MCP server make it the fastest way for SaaS founders and AI developers to ship subscription payments. Since 2015, Rebillia has helped businesses across ecommerce, software, and the emerging AI economy build and scale their subscription programs. For more information, visit rebillia.com.

About Global Payments
