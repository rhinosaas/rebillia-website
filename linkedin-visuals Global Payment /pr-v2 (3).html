Rebillia and Global Payments Join Forces to Power the Next Generation of Subscription Businesses

Partnership launches RebilliaPay -powered by Global Payments' PayFabric platform -as the preferred payment gateway for ecommerce merchants, SaaS companies, and AI-powered businesses, enabling multi-currency recurring billing, tokenized card vaulting, and preferred processing rates across 170+ countries

March 30, 2026

Rebillia, the adaptive subscription billing infrastructure platform for ecommerce, SaaS, and AI-native businesses, today announced a strategic partnership with Global Payments (NYSE: GPN), one of the world's leading payment technology companies. Under the partnership, Rebillia is launching RebilliaPay -its preferred payment gateway, powered by Global Payments' PayFabric -creating a single, turnkey solution for any business that wants to launch, manage, or scale subscription revenue.

The combination is designed to remove the biggest barriers businesses face when building recurring revenue: choosing the right billing platform and securing reliable, cost-effective payment processing. Now they can get both in one step. By starting with Rebillia, businesses automatically gain access to the most adaptive subscription billing platform on the market and to RebilliaPay - world-class payment processing backed by Global Payments' infrastructure that handles over 50 billion transactions per year across 170+ countries - creating the most powerful end-to-end subscription solution available today.

The partnership is equally compelling for businesses that already process payments through another gateway but find themselves limited by rigid subscription tooling. By moving to Rebillia, they gain the most adaptive subscription billing platform on the market while also unlocking preferred rates and world-class processing through Global Payments. It is a single switch that upgrades both their billing flexibility and their payment infrastructure at the same time.

For subscription businesses, payment reliability is only part of the picture. Industry data shows that 10 to 20 percent of annual subscriber churn stems from involuntary payment failures alone, but voluntary churn driven by rigid plans, poor timing, and lack of personalization can be just as costly. Rebillia and Global Payments tackle both sides together.

On the payments side, RebilliaPay's tokenized card vaulting ensures seamless recurring charges, while Rebillia's smart dunning engine automatically recovers failed payments before they become lost revenue. But Rebillia goes far beyond recovery. Its adaptive subscription platform uses AI-driven insights to encourage conversions, personalize offers based on customer behavior, and adjust plans to match what each subscriber actually needs. Customers can pause, swap, upgrade, or customize their subscriptions through a self-service portal, which keeps them engaged instead of pushing them to cancel. The result is fewer declines, higher conversion rates, stronger customer loyalty, and more predictable cash flow from day one.

Now, whether you're an ecommerce brand scaling recurring revenue, a SaaS founder growing to your next thousand users, an AI developer monetizing a new product, or a business that has outgrown its current subscription tools, you get world-class payments and the most adaptive billing platform on the market in a single integration. Why Businesses Are Choosing This Combination

RebilliaPay gives any subscription business, new or established, immediate access to:

  • Global payment processing: Accept recurring payments in 100+ currencies across 170+ countries. Businesses can sell internationally from day one without stitching together multiple payment integrations.
  • Higher payment success rates: Tokenized card vaulting ensures seamless recurring charges, while Rebillia's smart dunning engine automatically retries and recovers failed payments before they become lost revenue.
  • World-class security: PCI DSS Level 1 compliance (the highest certification level) plus built-in fraud prevention tools, so businesses of any size operate at the same security standard as the largest companies.
  • Preferred processing rates: Businesses that sign up with RebilliaPay receive preferred rates from Global Payments, lowering the cost of accepting recurring payments from the start.
  • One integrated stack: Subscription billing, payment gateway, dunning, analytics, and a customer self-service portal in a single platform. No middleware, no glue code, no separate vendor relationships to manage.

Built for Every Business Ready to Go Recurring

The partnership is designed to attract and serve businesses across three fast-growing segments:

  • Ecommerce brands running adaptive recurring commerce, subscription boxes, or automated fulfillment programs. Native to BigCommerce and AWS, and integrated with WooCommerce, Shopify, Magento, and Wix. With RebilliaPay, these merchants now get preferred gateway rates and global reach bundled in.
  • SaaS and digital services companies that need flexible recurring billing with usage-based pricing, multi-gateway support, and smart dunning. Instead of assembling a patchwork of tools, they can go live on a single, proven stack.
  • AI agents and vibe coders building the next wave of paid AI products. Rebillia's API-first architecture and MCP server integration let developers and AI-powered tools launch subscription payments in minutes. Combined with RebilliaPay, they get production-grade payment processing without the complexity.

Rebillia continues to support 7+ payment gateways including Stripe, PayPal, Authorize.net, Square, Braintree, and CyberSource, giving businesses the flexibility to add or optimize payment routing as they grow.

About Rebillia

Rebillia is adaptive subscription billing infrastructure for ecommerce, SaaS, and AI-native businesses. The platform enables businesses to launch and scale recurring revenue with AI-driven adaptive billing, subscription boxes, usage-based pricing, multi-gateway recurring payments, smart dunning, and a customer self-service portal. Rebillia integrates deeply with leading ecommerce platforms natively on BigCommerce (where it is the top-rated subscription app) and AWS, with integrations for WooCommerce, Shopify, Magento, and Wix, while its REST API and MCP server make it the fastest way for SaaS founders and AI developers to ship subscription payments. Since 2015, Rebillia has helped businesses across ecommerce, software, and the emerging AI economy build and scale their subscription programs. For more information, visit rebillia.com.
