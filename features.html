All plans include core subscription management, customer portal, and basic analytics. Smart dunning is available on Essentials and above. Advanced analytics, API access, and MCP server are available on Pro and Platform+ plans. See our pricing page for the full feature comparison.
Smart dunning is available on Essentials plans and above. It includes intelligent retry logic, customizable retry schedules, pre-dunning email notifications, and detailed recovery analytics to help you recover failed payments automatically.
API access is available on Pro and Platform+ plans. This includes full REST API access, webhooks, and the MCP server for AI integrations. Pro plans include generous rate limits, while Platform+ offers custom rate limits and priority support.
Yes, the portal is fully brandable with your logo, colors, and custom domain. Customers can manage subscriptions, update payment methods, view billing history, and modify their plans. The portal is mobile-responsive and works on all devices.
Yes, usage-based billing is available on Pro and Platform+ plans. You can meter usage, set thresholds, configure overage pricing, and combine usage-based charges with flat-rate subscriptions for hybrid billing models.
Basic analytics are included on all plans. Advanced analytics including MRR dashboards, churn analysis, cohort reports, customer lifetime value, and revenue forecasting are available on Pro and Platform+ plans. All data is exportable via CSV or API.
