OUR STORY

From BigCommerce App to Agent-Native Monetization

Rebillia started in 2015 with a conviction: subscription billing was broken. Rigid. Built for a world where humans managed every pricing decision and billing change manually. We started by building a better subscription app for BigCommerce - and quickly became the top-rated app on the marketplace.

But we saw something bigger. Billing isn't just payment collection - it's the control layer of the customer relationship. Every pause, upgrade, swap, and cancellation is a signal. Every billing cycle is a decision point. We rebuilt our platform around this insight.

Today, Rebillia is agent-native monetization infrastructure. Programmable pricing engines, autonomous lifecycle automation, monetization experimentation frameworks, and credit systems - all designed to be operated by AI agents. Built on real ecommerce experience, designed for the era where software agents run the revenue layer of digital businesses.

About Rebillia

Rebillia was founded in 2015 with the mission of making subscription billing accessible to businesses of all sizes. We started on BigCommerce and have since expanded to WooCommerce and custom platforms via API.
Rebillia's key partners include Global Payments, one of the world's largest payment technology companies, BigCommerce as our primary ecommerce platform partner (where Rebillia is the #1 subscription app), and IntuitSolutions, a leading BigCommerce design and development agency. These partnerships provide world-class payment processing, deep platform integration, and expert implementation services.
Over 80 businesses trust Rebillia for their subscription billing needs, spanning ecommerce, SaaS, and professional services industries. Our platform maintains a 4.9/5 rating from merchants.
Rebillia natively integrates with BigCommerce and WooCommerce. For any other platform, our REST API and developer tools enable custom integrations. We also offer an MCP server for AI-powered subscription management.
