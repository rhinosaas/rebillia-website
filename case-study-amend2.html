How Amend2 Turned a Physical Product Into Recurring Revenue

How a firearm accessories manufacturer built a collectible subscription program that generates 30% of monthly revenue with Rebillia on BigCommerce.

Firearm Accessories BigCommerce Subscribe & Save
ABOUT THE COMPANY
Built on Rebillia's agent-native monetization infrastructure

Company Overview

Amend2 is a U.S.-based manufacturer specializing in firearm accessories, including magazines, grips, and other non-regulated components. Known for durable polymer magazines and distinctive designs, the company serves a large community of firearm enthusiasts, collectors, and sport shooters. Their products are primarily sold through e-commerce and retail partners. As the market matured, Amend2 began exploring ways to deepen customer engagement and create more predictable revenue beyond traditional one-time purchases.

THE CHALLENGE

Revenue Volatility from One-Time Purchases

Like many manufacturers selling physical products online, Amend2 faced revenue volatility. Most sales occurred as individual purchases driven by promotions, product launches, or seasonal demand — making long-term revenue forecasting difficult.

  • Revenue dependent on promotions and product launches with unpredictable demand cycles
  • Highly engaged customer base with frequent repeat purchases — but no recurring structure
  • Strong interest in limited-edition designs, but no way to monetize that enthusiasm predictably
  • Managing recurring billing, order renewals for different products each month would be unmanageable manually
THE SOLUTION

Collectible Subscription Program with Rebillia

Amend2 partnered with Rebillia to launch a subscription program centered around limited-edition magazine designs released every month. Subscribers receive priority access to newly designed magazines before the general public, creating both exclusivity and predictable recurring demand.

Subscribe & Save

Monthly subscription delivering a uniquely designed magazine each month. Subscribers get guaranteed access to limited-edition designs and priority fulfillment before public inventory.

Smart Dunning

Automated payment retry and intelligent recovery scheduling. Ensures subscribers aren't lost due to temporary payment issues while reducing operational burden on the team.

Self-Serve Customer Portal

Subscribers manage their memberships through Rebillia's self-service portal — update payment methods, pause or modify subscriptions, and manage shipping details.
