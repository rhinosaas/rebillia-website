Monetization Patterns for Every Business Model

Rebillia's agent-native architecture supports any monetization pattern — from simple recurring orders to autonomous AI-operated pricing, retention, and experimentation workflows.

Rebillia is agent-native monetization infrastructure that helps ecommerce, SaaS, and AI-operated businesses launch and manage revenue models — including subscribe-and-save, subscription boxes, usage-based billing, dynamic pricing, and autonomous lifecycle management — across BigCommerce, WooCommerce, and custom platforms via API.

Billing Components

Every subscription is built from composable parts: charges, schedules, rules, entitlements.

Lifecycle Engine

AI agents respond to lifecycle events — activation, pause, upgrade, churn risk — with autonomous retention actions, pricing adjustments, and credit grants.

Shared Control

Customers, merchants, and AI agents all participate in monetization — agents run experiments and retention actions within governance boundaries you define.
