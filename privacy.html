Rebillia’s Online Privacy Policy

By using Rebillia Platform Software, you agree to our Privacy Policy.

By Using Rebillia Platform Software, you agree to our Privacy and that you have read our Online Privacy Policy.

By visiting rebillia.com, or our other user interfaces, you are accepting the practices described in this Privacy Policy (the “Privacy Policy”).

For subscription and billing data processed on behalf of our merchant clients, Rebillia acts as a Data Processor. For information collected directly through our own website and client accounts, Rebillia acts as a Data Controller.

This Privacy Policy operates in conjunction with our Rebillia Terms and Conditions which together constitute the entire agreement between you and Rebillia. Please review each of these documents to understand all of the terms and conditions of your agreement with us.

Rebillia may contain links to third-party websites. Rebillia is not responsible for the information practices or the content of such third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of third-party websites to understand their information practices.

Automatic information, such as computer sign-on data, browser type, browser language, statistics on page views, traffic to and from our user interfaces and ad data is stored within the Rebillia system. Like many websites, we use identifiers such as “cookies” and Web beacons to obtain this automatic information.

This Privacy Policy concentrates on that information that is “individually identifiable” information that an individual or customer reasonably expects to be kept private. As the term suggests, individually identifiable information is information that can be associated with a specific individual or entity, such as name, address, telephone number, e-mail address and/or information about online activities directly linked to them.

Rebillia does not store full credit-card numbers or sensitive payment data. We may store tokenized payment information (such as last four digits, cardholder name, and expiration date) securely for subscription and billing purposes.

Rebillia has developed the following online privacy policy to protect individually identifiable information. This policy covers Rebillia and its affiliates and applies to all individually identifiable information that Rebillia obtains when a customer registers for or uses a Rebillia online service, including visiting a Rebillia owned and controlled Web site.

How Rebillia Protects Your Information

Rebillia will not sell, trade or disclose to third parties any individually identifiable information derived from the registration for or use of a Rebillia online service without the consent of the customer (except as required by subpoena, search warrant or other legal process or in the case of imminent physical harm to the customer or others). Rebillia may share personal information with trusted service providers (sub-processors) that perform functions on our behalf, consistent with this Privacy Policy and our Data Processing Addendum. When Rebillia uses other agents, contractors or companies to perform services on its behalf, Rebillia will endeavor to reasonably ensure that the company protects your individually identifiable information consistent with this policy. If Rebillia includes your name and any other individually identifiable information in a directory that Rebillia creates from information we receive as an online service provider, the company will give you the opportunity to have your information excluded from that directory. Business directories that Rebillia creates may, however, contain similar information obtained from other sources.

Rebillia will collect and use individually identifiable information for billing purposes, to anticipate and resolve problems with your service, or to create and inform you of products and services that better meet your needs. This means that Rebillia may contact merchant clients about new features and services. We do not use end-customer data for marketing or advertising purposes. We will not disclose your individually identifiable information to third parties who want to market products to you without your prior consent.

A customer may choose not to receive direct marketing communications from Rebillia or its affiliates or customers in connection with Rebillia online services. Upon such choice, Rebillia (a) will not contact that customer directly with marketing messages about Rebillia online services, and (b) will not use individually identifiable information obtained from that customer’s registration for use of an online service to contact that customer with marketing messages about any Rebillia products or services. A customer may choose not to receive such messages by e-mail, by mail or by telephone.

Individually identifiable information that a visitor volunteers at one of the Rebillia Web sites to order Rebillia services, or the services of Rebillia affiliates, other than online services, will be protected just as if the information had been provided under more traditional ways of ordering that service.

Rebillia may use third party service providers to provide certain services to you and we may share personal information with such service providers. We require any company with which we may share personal information to protect that data in a manner consistent with this policy and to limit the use of such personal information to the performance of services for Rebillia.

Rebillia servers automatically gather information about which sites customers visit on the Internet and which pages are visited within a Rebillia Web site. The company does not use that information except in the aggregate.

As part of your registration on our Website to use the Products or Services, our Website, you agree that you may receive certain administrative communications from us, such as administrative announcements and customer service messages regarding Products or Services and products during the period that we provide Products or Services to you. You may not opt out of receiving such administrative communications.

Rebillia has implemented technology and security features that meet or exceed industry standards, and has adopted strict policy guidelines to safeguard the privacy of your individually identifiable information from unauthorized access or improper use, and we will continue to enhance our security procedures as new technology becomes available.

The company will not read or disclose to third parties private e-mail communications that are transmitted using Rebillia services except as required to operate the service or as otherwise authorized by law.

Rebillia may use individually identifiable information to investigate and help prevent potentially unlawful activity or activity that threatens the network or otherwise violates the customer agreement for that service.

Rebillia will retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill legal obligations. Data related to merchant accounts is deleted or anonymized within 30 days of account termination, unless retention is required by law.

Rebillia honors requests from customers for account information, such as name, address, or billing information, and will correct any such information that is inaccurate. Customers may verify that appropriate corrections have been made.

Rebillia may include community forums, blogs, or chat rooms. Any other visitors to these forums may read, collect, or use the personal information you choose to disclose there, and may use it to send you unsolicited messages. Rebillia is not responsible for the privacy of information you choose to disclose in these forums.

If you are a consumer with concerns about the Rebillia online privacy policy or its implementation, you may contact us at help@rebillia.com.

For information about how Rebillia processes data on behalf of merchants, please see our Data Processing Addendum.

Rebillia will keep this policy current and will inform you of any changes that we make.

Additional Privacy Protections for Children Using the Internet

Rebillia joins the industry in recognizing that children, including young teens, may not be in a position to make informed choices about personal information requested online.

Accordingly, Rebillia does not target children or teenagers (younger than 18) for collection of information online. Rebillia does not solicit or collect individually identifiable information targeted at children and teenagers under 18 and does not allow anyone else to do so on a Rebillia Web site. In addition, on all of its online services and Rebillia Web sites, Rebillia will encourage children to seek the consent of their parents before providing any information about themselves or their households to anyone on the Internet.

Finally, Rebillia encourages parents to take an active role to protect the privacy and security of their children and to prevent the inappropriate use of information about their children. Rebillia supports the development of technologies that help parents control the collection and use of personal information from children who use online services in their households.
